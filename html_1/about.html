About Us - Waterbury Church of God 7th Day

At Waterbury Church of God 7th Day, we are a Christ-centered, Bible-believing community dedicated to worshiping God, serving others, and growing in faith together. Located in the heart of Waterbury, Connecticut, our church family gathers every Sabbath to celebrate God's goodness, study His Word, and build one another up through fellowship and prayer. We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit, the importance of community, and the hope found in Jesus Christ. Women's Ministry Statement

Our Womens Ministry plays a vital role in our church life, offering support, connection, and purpose for women of all ages. Our mission is to serve our homes, communities, and show Christs love through our actions. We aim to reflect faith, compassion, and service, inspiring others to see Christ through the way we live. We strive to show kindness, character, and leadership while also helping women discover their gifts and live out Gods purpose.
